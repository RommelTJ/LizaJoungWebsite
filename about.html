Hi! My name is Liza Joung (pronounced Lee-za) and I am a maker. I sketch, I design, I research, I knit, I podcast, I video-edit, I take pictures, etc. Originally hailing from the land of liquid sunshine (Portland, Oregon) I am now located in permanently sunny San Diego, California and travel frequently to Tijuana, Mexico. I am an introvert, glasses-wearer, and an Apple products owner. I graduated from Smith College in May of 2013 with a B.A. in American Studies. I've always been creative and decided recently that a career making things would be best suited for my unique gifts and temperment. I am currently available for work so let me know if you'd like to work together! Contact me.
